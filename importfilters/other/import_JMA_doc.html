Hypocenter record (96bytes)-----------------------------------------

Cols. Type Description

1 A1 Header (Agency Code). Coded as

J: Hypocenter record determined by JMA

(This code should be written)

I: Hypocenter record determined by ISC

U: Hypocenter record determined by USGS

C: Comment record

- Origin time (Japan Standard Time = UT + 9h) -

2 I4 Years

3 I2 Months

4 I2 Days

5 I2 Hours

6 I2 Minutes

7 F4.2 Seconds

8 F4.2 Standard error for origin time (Seconds)

- Hypocenter -

9 I3 Latitude (Degrees, North+ and South-)

10 F4.2 (Minutes)

11 F4.2 Standard error for latitude (Minutes)

　

12 I4 Longitude (Degrees, East+ and West-)

13 F4.2 (Minutes)

14 F4.2 Standard error for longitude (Minutes)

　

15 F5.2 Depth in kilometers (Depth-free method) This method computes the hypocenter with the initial condition of the depth as unknown
or
I3,2X Depth in kilometers (depth-sliced method) This method assigns the depths changing by 1 kilometer increments.

16 F3.2 Standard error for depth (kilometers)

　

- Magnitude -

　

17 F2.1 Magnitude 1

18 A1 Type of magnitude 1.

J: JMA magnitude

B: Mb - USGS or ISC body wave magnitude

S: Ms - USGS or ISC surface wave magnitude

19 F2.1 Magnitude 2

20 A1 Type of magnitude 2.

B: Mb - USGS or ISC body wave magnitude

S: Ms - USGS or ISC surface wave magnitude

- Other Parameters

21 A1 Type of travel time table.

1: Standard table

2: Table of far east off the Sanriku district

3: Table of the east off Hokkaido district

4: Table of the regions of southern part of the Kurile Islands 

blank: Determined by another agency

　

22 A1 Precision of hypocenter

1: Depth-free method

2: Depth-sliced method

3: Fixed depth

4: Using depth phase

7: Poor solution

8: Not determined or not accepted

　

23 A1 Subsidiary information of the event.

1: Natural earthquake

2: Insufficient number of JMA station

3: Artificial event

4: Noise

　

24 A1 Maximum Seismic Intensity (JMA scale)

　

25 A1 Damage class (after Utsu)

1: Slight damage (cracks on walls and ground)

2: Light damaged (broken houses, roads, etc.)

3: 2 - 19 persons killed or 2 - 999 houses completely destroyed

4: 20 - 199 persons killed or 1,000 - 9,999 houses completely destroyed

5: 200 - 1,999 persons killed or 10,000 - 99,999 houses completely destroyed

6: 2,000 - 19,999 persons killed or 100,000 - 999,999 houses completely destroyed

7: 20,000 or more persons killed or 1,000,000 or more houses completely destroyed

　

26 A1 Tsunami class (after Imamura and Iida)

Tsunami height Damage

1: 50cm none

2: 1m very slight damage

3: 2m slight damage to coastal areas and ships

4: 4- 6m human injures

5: 10-20m damage to more than 400km of coastline

6: 30m damage to more than 500km of coastline

　

27 I1 District number of epicenter

28 I3 Geographical region number of epicenter

29 A24 Geographical region name of epicenter

30 I3 Number of observations contributing to hypocenter determination (After and from 1983) or Number of phase data employed  in hypocenter determination (Before 1983 if indicated)

31 A1 Hypocenter determination flag depends on JMA computer system
