NIED Kanto-Tokai catalog

Download URL: http://www.bosai.go.jp/center/kanto-tokai/index_e.html

To retrieve the catalog

Change to the importfilters/KantoTokai directory

Call gettokaicatalog directory

The script retrieves all necessary data and converts it into fixed-column format to be used with this importfilter and stores it into directory. The script uses sed and xsltproc.

Explanation of mechanism solution

The contents of mechanism solution data is as following.

IDNO JYMD IOTH IOTM OTS HLAT HLNG HDEP FMAMP JDMAMP NPOL NERR RANKPX AZP THP RANKTX AZT THT SCORE STRIK1 DIP1 SLIP1 STRIK2 DIP2 SLIP2

IDNO ID No.
JYMD origin time, ymd(JST)
IOTH origin time, hour
IOTM origin time, minute
OTS origin time, second
HLAT latitude of hypocenter
HLNG longitude of hypocenter
HDEP hypocentral depth
FMAMP magnitude
JDMAMP index of magnitude
NPOL number of station whose polarity is detected
NERR number of station whose polarity doesn't match solution
RANKPX rank of P axes
AZP azimuth of P axes(N->E)
THP theta of P axes(down->up)
RANKTX rank of T axes
AZT azimuth of T axes(N->E)
THT theta of T axes(down->up)
SCORE score of mechanism solution
STRIK1 strike of nodal plane1
DIP1 dip angle of nodal plane1
SLIP1 slip of nodal plane1
STRIK2 strike of nodal plane2
DIP2 dip angle of nodal plane2
SLIP2 slip of nodal plane2
